# Racial Mechanics Roadmap

## Overview

Racial Mechanics covers the innate traits, speeds, and resistances derived from a character's species.

## 🟢 Alpha (MVP) _(Implemented)_

**Focus**: Stat Blocks.

- **Speed**: Base walking speed validation.
- **Size**: Controls Token footprint (Medium 1x1, Small 1x1).
- **Traits**: Simple passive listing (e.g., "Darkvision" - checked by renderer).

## 🟡 Beta

**Focus**: Active Racial Traits.

- **Dragonborn**: Breath Weapon AoE actions (Scaling damage).
- **Tiefling**: Hellish Rebuke reaction support.
- **Gnome**: Magic Resistance (Advantage on INT/WIS/CHA saves vs Magic).
- **Halfling**: "Lucky" (Auto-reroll 1s in Engine Dice Resolution).

## 🔵 V1 (Release)

**Focus**: Exotic & Flying.

- **Aarakocra/Owlin**: 3D Flight mechanics (Z-axis movement validation).
- **Changeling**: Shapechanger logic (Appearance/Token swap).
- **Dwarf**: Stonecunning (Passive history check boost on Stone origins).
